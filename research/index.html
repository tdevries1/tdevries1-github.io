Publications

  • Tjeerd de Vries and Alexis Toda . Capital and Labor Income Pareto Exponents across Time and Space. (2021), Accepted at Review of Income and Wealth
    [link]
    Estimate capital and labor income Pareto exponents across 475 country-year observations and document that capital income inequality is higher than labor income inequality (median Pareto exponents 1.46 and 3.35 respectively) and the two inequalities are uncorrelated, suggesting importance of distinguishing the two.
